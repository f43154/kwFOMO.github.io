Welcome to
kwFOMO

The journey of satisfying my Fear of Missing Out in Kitchener-Waterloo

The goal behind this blog is to answer the question, "What do you want to do this weekend?" Depending on who you ask, KW is both an exciting place to live and an incredibly boring one. I hope to collect information about activities, businesses, events and associations to help people learn more about what this place has to offer!
